Hospitality Recruitment
Across Every Sector

Hospitality is diverse, and so is Flok.

We connect venues with skilled, verified hospitality professionals across the UK, from fine dining restaurants and luxury hotels to pubs, events, contract catering, and specialist operations. Whether you need chefs, bartenders, servers, front-of-house teams, or back-of-house support, Flok helps you find the right people faster.

Fine Dining &
Luxury Hospitality

Exceptional venues need exceptional staff.
Flok supports:

  • Michelin-Star Restaurants - Head chefs, sous chefs, and experienced kitchen teams
  • Fine Dining & AA Rosette Venues - Front-of-house professionals, wine waiters, chefs, and senior kitchen staff
  • Luxury Hotels & 5-Star Resorts - Hotel operations, concierge, guest services, chefs, and food and beverage teams
  • Private Hospitality - Private chefs for households, superyachts, chalets, and exclusive events

Find experienced hospitality professionals who understand luxury
service, precision, and high standards.

Pubs, Restaurants
& Casual Dining

The heart of UK hospitality needs reliable people. Flok supplies staff for:

  • Gastro Pubs & Traditional Pubs - Bar staff, kitchen teams, and pub managers
  • Restaurant Chains & Independent Eateries - Servers, hosts, and kitchen porters
  • Cafés & Coffee Shops - Baristas, café assistants, and shift supervisors
  • Fast-Casual Dining - Line cooks, food prep staff, and customer service teams

Flexible hospitality staff for busy venues that need quality and consistency.

Events & Corporate
Hospitality

Fast-paced events need people you can trust. We provide staff for:

  • Weddings & Private Events - Event servers, bartenders, and catering teams
  • Festivals & Stadium Catering - High-volume food service and beverage staff
  • Corporate Events & Conferences - Polished hospitality professionals and event teams
  • Exhibition & Trade Show Catering - Experienced front-of-house and catering staff

Vetted hospitality professionals, ready for any event size or setting.

Institutional
& Contract Catering

Reliable staffing for essential services. Flok covers:

  • Healthcare Catering - Hospitals, care homes, and medical facilities
  • Education Catering - Schools, colleges, universities, and student dining
  • Government & Public Services - Military bases, prisons, and public institutions
  • Corporate Dining - Office canteens, executive dining, and workplace catering

Specialist hospitality recruitment for structured, high-volume,
and operationally critical environments.

Travel & Specialist
Hospitality

Some hospitality roles need adaptable people in unique environments. Flok recruits for:

  • Cruise Ships & Airlines - Galley teams, onboard hospitality, and dining staff
  • Railway Catering - Train service staff and mobile hospitality teams
  • Street Food & Food Trucks - Mobile catering professionals and event vendors
  • Film & TV Location Catering - Production catering teams
  • Remote and specialist locations - Offshore sites, holiday parks, resorts, and community kitchens

Adaptable hospitality professionals for any location, service style, or environment.
