No agencies. No faff. Hospitality hiring, sorted.

Flok is the UK's only hiring platform built by hospitality people, for hospitality people.

We connect verified chefs, bartenders, and front/back-of-house staff directly with businesses — instantly. No contracts, no commissions, no middlemen.

The old way is slow, costly, and impersonal. Flok is fast, fair, and transparent — putting control back in the hands of the people who make hospitality work.

Why Flok?

  • Built from real hospitality experience — not generic tech or outdated recruitment models.
  • Verified candidates only with clear profiles, availability and reviews.
  • Pay-as-you-go model — no long-term contracts or commission charges.
  • Smart matching — powered by tech, led by people.

Designed to bring speed, trust and fairness back into hiring.

We're not another recruitment agency. We're not another job board.

We're Flok, and we're building the future of hospitality hiring.

Who's behind Flok?

Founder and Director,
Jack Day

Flok was founded by Jack Day, a chef and hospitality professional with over 25 years of industry experience.

Jack has worked in Michelin-starred kitchens, led teams at high-end events, catered for luxury brands, corporate clients and even trained at prestigious institutions like Hambleton Hall and The Ritz, London.

He built a successful recruitment agency from scratch, gaining firsthand knowledge of what works and what doesn't in hiring hospitality staff.

This unique blend of on-the-ground experience and recruitment insight led to one clear conclusion: the current system is broken. It's outdated, inefficient and far too impersonal for both employers and jobseekers.

Flok isn't a trend or a tech side project. It's the product of real experience, years in the field and a quiet mission to create something better.

A platform built not just for the industry but by someone who's lived it.

Founder and Director, Jack Day
